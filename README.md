# HeritageLens AI

### AI-Powered Heritage Discovery & Preservation Platform

**Preserving Cultural Heritage Through Artificial Intelligence.**

---

## Overview

HeritageLens AI is an AI-powered platform designed to make cultural heritage more accessible, interactive, and educational. Using Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision, the platform identifies heritage sites and artifacts, retrieves contextual information, and provides meaningful historical insights that promote heritage awareness and preservation.

---

## Problem Statement

Cultural heritage sites and artifacts hold immense historical and educational value, yet many remain poorly documented, difficult to understand, or inaccessible to wider audiences.

Visitors often lack contextual information, while educational institutions and heritage organizations face challenges in delivering engaging and interactive learning experiences.

There is a growing need for intelligent digital solutions that bridge the gap between cultural heritage and modern technology by making historical knowledge more accessible, informative, and engaging.

---

## Solution

HeritageLens AI leverages Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision to recognize heritage assets and generate contextual insights.

The platform enables users to:

- Identify heritage sites and artifacts through image recognition
- Access historical and cultural information instantly
- Explore interactive educational experiences
- Promote heritage awareness and preservation
- Improve accessibility to cultural knowledge through technology

## How It Works

1. Upload an image or scan a heritage asset.
2. The AI recognition engine analyzes the image.
3. The system identifies the heritage asset.
4. Historical and contextual information is retrieved.
5. Interactive insights and educational content are presented to the user.

The Problem

Why HeritageLens AI?

Millions of people visit historical landmarks every year but often leave without understanding their true historical, cultural, and architectural significance.

Information boards are limited, guides are not always available, and cultural stories are gradually being forgotten in the digital age. HeritageLens AI bridges this gap by making heritage exploration interactive, intelligent, and universally accessible.
